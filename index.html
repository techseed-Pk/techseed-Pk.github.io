About Techseed

Techseed provides innovative software and web development services at affordable rates, focusing primarily on building long term business partnerships based on unwavering trust that values human connection more than anything.

With years of experience as both independent contractors and subcontractors, we've helped numerous businesses achieve their goals. We prefer to collaborate with European clients due to our compatible time zone (Pakistan’s EST-friendly hours), ensuring seamless and effective communication.
